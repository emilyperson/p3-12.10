Pan-Friend Salmon Steaks

These pan-fried salmon steaks are a heart-healthy alternative to steak night. Serve with roasted potatoes and a green salad for a well-balanced meal. Pairs wonderfully with a Pinot Noir! Recipe adapted from Healthy Recipes Blog.

Ingredients

  • 2 (6 oz) salmon steaks
  • dash of salt and pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried sage
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • lemon for serving

Preparation

  1. Mix seasoning in a small bowl
  2. Rub salmon steaks with seasoning
  3. Melt butter in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Fry salmon steaks for 3 minutes on first side
  5. Flip salmons and fry about 3 more minutes or until cooked to desired doneness
