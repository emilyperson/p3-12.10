Thai Red Curry with Vegetables

This is a healthy recipe that is easy to make at home! You can sub in different vegetables, you just need about 3 cups total. Cookie and Kate.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, finely minced
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 red bell peppers, sliced thinly
  • 3 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 2 tablespoons Thai red curry paste
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 1/2 cups thinly sliced lacinato kale (tough ribs removed)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon coconut aminos
  • 2 teaspoons rice vinegar
  • cooked rice for serving

Preparation

  1. Heat coconut oil in a large skillet or wok. Add onion and salt and cook until translucent. Add garlic and ginger and cook about 30 more seconds, stirring constantly.
  2. Add bell peppers and cook until tender, 3-5 minutes. Then add the curry paste and stir for 2 more minutes.
  3. Add coconut milk, kale, sugar, and water. Bring to a simmer and cook for about 10 minutes, until the kale is softened to taste.
  4. Remove pan from heat and add the coconut aminos and rice vinegar
  5. Serve with cooked rice
