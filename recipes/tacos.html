Ground Beef Tacos

Margaritas may be the classic pairing with tacos, but if you're in the mood for wine, Zinfandel goes nicely with ground beef tacos. Cooking them in the instant pot makes taco night very easy! Recipe adapted from Coco Morante's Ultimate Instant Pot Cookbook.

Ingredients

Taco filling

  • 1 tablespoon avocado oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1/2 jalapeño
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 3 tablespoons chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 cup broth or red wine
  • dash of salt
  • tortillas for serving

Optional Add-Ons

  • shredded cheese
  • salsa
  • diced tomatoes
  • cilantro

Preparation

  1. Set Instant Pot to sauté and add avocado oil. Cook the onion with a dash of salt until softened. Add pepper and garlic and cook 1 more minute
  2. Add ground beef and cook for 3 more minutes. Add spices, broth or wine, and another dash of salt.
  3. Seal Instant Pot and set Pressure Cook to high for 10 minutes. (The pot will take about 10 minutes to come up to pressure before the cooking program begins.)
  4. When the cooking program ends, release the pressure, open the pot, and stir. Add more seasoning as needed.
  5. Assemble tacos and serve!
