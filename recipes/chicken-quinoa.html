Autumn Chicken Quinoa Bowl

This autumnal chicken quinoa bowl is light and filling! The warmth of the butternut squash and spices pairs nicely with a Côtes du Rhône. Recipe adapted from Two Cooks in the Kitchen.

Ingredients

Butternut Squash

  • 3 cups butternut squash, peeled and cut into 1-inch chunks
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • salt and pepper to taste

Chicken

  • 1 pound chicken, cut into 1-inch chunks
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Quinoa

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 2/3 cup water with 1/2 teaspoon salt

Dressing

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
  • 1/2 tablespoon honey
  • dash of cinnamon
  • salt and pepper to taste

Optional Add-Ons

  • baby spinach
  • dried cherries
  • toasted pecans
  • pumpkin seeds

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 425F. Line a pan with parchment. Or use foil and spray it with oil.
  2. Roast the squash. Toss butternut squash with oil, maple syrup, salt and pepper in a bowl. Place squash in single layer on pan. Roast squash for 20-30 minutes until tender and slightly caramelized, tossing or flipping over half way through.
  3. Rinse quinoa in a mesh strainer. Place in medium pot with 1 2/3 cups water and 1/2 teaspoon salt. Bring to a boil, then lower heat to simmer. Cook for 15 minutes. Turn off heat and let it sit for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork.
  4. Cook chicken. Heat oil in pan and cook the chicken pieces in seasoning until cooked through.
  5. Make Dressing. Put all ingredients in a mason jar and shake with the lid on.
  6. Assemble the bowl ingredients and serve!
