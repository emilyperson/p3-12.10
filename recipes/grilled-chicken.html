Grilled Chicken Salad

A healthy option year-round, this grilled chicken can be cooked on an outdoor grill or stove top. The dressing also doubles as a marinade in this recipe. Be sure to marinate at least an hour to get the most flavor! The salty flavor of the parmigiano reggiano pairs nicely with a cold Pinot Grigio. Recipe adapted from Dinner at the Zoo.

Ingredients

Salad

  • 1 pound chicken breast
  • 6 cups romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 3/4 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 3/4 cup persian cucumbers
  • 1 avocado, peeled and sliced
  • fresh parmigiano reggiano, shredded or shaved

Marinade/Dressing

  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 3 tablespoons white wine vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons finely diced shallots
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Make the dressing. Mix all dressing ingredients in a small bowl. Reserve half for the dressing and use the other half to marinate the chicken. Let chicken marinate for at least an hour and up to 8 hours.
  2. Grill chicken, either on outdoor grill or on the stove on medium-high. Let chicken rest 5 minutes and slice.
  3. Put lettuce in a large bowl and add the rest of the dressing.
  4. Arrange the grilled chicken and other salad ingredients in the bowl with the lettuce. Sprinkle with parmigiano reggiano and serve!
