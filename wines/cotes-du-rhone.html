Côtes du Rhône

Also known as GSM Blend, Côtes du Rhône consists of three grapes from Southern France: Grenache, Syrah, and Mourvedre. Notes of baking spaces and herbs go well with Mediterranean food. Try pairing this wine with our Autumn Chicken Quinoa Bowl.

Features

  • Medium body
  • Medium acidity
  • Medium tannin
  • Low sweetness

How to Serve

  • Oversized glass
  • Room temperature
  • Decant 30 minutes
