Zinfandel

Zinfandel is fruit-forward and bold, and it pairs with a variety of cuisine from Moroccan to Asian barbecue. We like to have it for taco night! Try pairing this wine with our Ground Beef Tacos.

Features

  • Medium body
  • Low acidity
  • Medium tannin
  • Medium sweetness

How to Serve

  • Red glass
  • Room temperature
  • Decant 30 minutes
