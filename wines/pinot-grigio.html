Pinot Grigio

Pinot Grigio, also known as Pinot Gris, is a pink grape mutation of Pinot Noir and has a fresh, light taste. It is most often paired with with meats, seafood, and citrus-based dishes. Try pairing this wine with our Grilled Chicken Salad.

Features

  • Light body
  • Medium acidity
  • Low tannin
  • Medium sweetness

How to Serve

  • White glass
  • Cold
  • Do not decant
