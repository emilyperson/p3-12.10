Riesling

Riesling turns away a lot of people because of how sweet it can be. Give it a chance with Indian or Asian food, because it makes such an interesting pairing! It is also surprisingly nice with bacon. Try pairing this wine with our Honey Garlic Shrimp.

Features

  • Light body
  • High acidity
  • Low tannin
  • High sweetness

How to Serve

  • White glass
  • Cold
  • Do not decant
