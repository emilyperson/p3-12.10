Pinot Noir

Pinot Noir is one of the most popular light red wines, and it is often paired with chicken, mushrooms, and Thanksgiving turkeys. It is also one of the few red wines that goes well with seafood! Red-flesh fish like salmon, ruby trout, and meaty snapper are all great pairings for Pinot Noir. Try pairing this wine with our Pan-fried Salmon Steaks.

Features

  • Light body
  • Medium acidity
  • Low tannin
  • Low sweetness

How to Serve

  • Aroma Collector glass
  • Cellar temperature
  • Decant 30 minutes
